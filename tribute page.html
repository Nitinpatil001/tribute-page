Chatrapati Shivajiraje Bhonsale

1630-1680

"Self-confidence Provides Strength and Power Import Knowledge. Knowledge Provides Stability and Stability Leads to Victory."

Biography

(Shivaji Bhonsle: 19 February 1630 - 3 April 1680), also referred to as Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, was an Indian ruler and a member of the Bhonsle Maratha clan. Eventually, Shivaji carved out his own independent kingdom from the declining Adilshahi sultanate of Bijapur which formed the genesis of the Maratha Empire. In 1674, he was formally crowned the Chhatrapati of his realm at Raigad Fort.
